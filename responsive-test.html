Testing in Browser DevTools

  1. Open DevTools: Press F12 or Ctrl+Shift+I (Cmd+Opt+I on Mac)
  2. Enable Device Toolbar: Press Ctrl+Shift+M (Cmd+Shift+M on Mac)
  3. Select "Responsive" from the device dropdown
  4. Drag the viewport edges or enter specific dimensions
  5. Test these key widths: 375px, 768px, 1366px, 1920px, 2560px
